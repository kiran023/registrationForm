It is the official quizzing society of MANIT, whose aim is to promote quizzing and group discussions amongst the student community.

We are a team of Quizzers. Designers, Content Writers, Executives, Photographers, Video Editors, Web developers and bloggers. You can still join our team even if you are not good at quizzing. One just needs to be passionate to be a part of QCM.

The link for registration is available in the bio of The Instagram handle of QCM.

Self Confidence and a positive attitude are all you need.

A blogger has to work on the advancement of the blog site of QCM has to write blogs which are informative, unique and dynamic.

The recruitments will be held in 2 rounds, First round will kickstart on 6 July Written test for Quizzers, Executives and Content Writers will be held on 6 July, the results of which will be announced on 7 July. Candidates qualifying in round 1, will proceed to round 2 for a Personal Interview.

The link for the written test will be sent to your Email Id.

The exam will consist of questions on basic aptitude and general knowledge.

Video Editors, Designers and Photographers are required to submit portfolios.

All the students of MANIT are eligible to join QCM
